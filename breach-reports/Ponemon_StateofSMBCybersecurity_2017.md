How SMBs can protect against cyber threats:

Provide cybersecurity training to all employees — especially related to phishing attacks.

Adopt password management software to gain visibility and control over password security.

Extend protections to employee-owned mobile devices.

Invest in your cybersecurity now so you don’t have to pay more later.

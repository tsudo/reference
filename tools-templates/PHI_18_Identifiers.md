# PHI_18_Identifiers

Original format: `docx`

18 identifiers that comprise PHI

Names

Addresses (other than Town or City, State, and Zip)

Dates (other than year directly related to an individual's birth, admission, discharge, death)

Telephone numbers

Fax numbers

E-mail addresses

Social security numbers

Medical record numbers

Health plan beneficiary numbers

Account numbers

Certificate/License numbers

Vehicle identifiers and serial numbers (including license plate)

Device identifiers and serial numbers

Web URLs (Uniform Resource Locators)

Internet Protocol (IP) addresses

Biometric identifiers (including finger and voice prints)

Full face photography, any comparable images

Any other unique identifying number, characteristic or code
